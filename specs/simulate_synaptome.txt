1. Navigate to Workflows page with simulate activity filter
2. Click Simulate category → Synaptome card
3. Model picker: click Public tab, verify table has rows

5. Close filter panel.
6. Click random row (skip excluded rows)
7. Mini-detail: verify title, description, 2 thumbnails/images, metadata, close (x) button, "View details" button are present, click "Use model"
8. Config page: wait for layout, verify Configuration + Results tabs
9. Wait for 3D morphology viewer (non-blocking, 120s timeout)
10. Info tab: fill datetime as name, description "automated test of synaptome"
11. Verify Registered by and Registered at are not empty
12. Click through Experimental setup tab, verify labels present
13. Click Synaptic input, verify at least 1 synaptic input is displayed
14. In the synaptic input, click on the eye button, the 3D view should display the synapses, the eye button, should be crossed out.
15. Stimulation protocol: wait for plot, verify download button clickable
16. Recording tab: add recordings for available sections (soma, dend, apic, axon)
17. Click Run experiment
18. Results tab: verify auto-redirect, left menu has All + recording buttons
19. Verify Download CSV and Reconfigure disabled while running
20. Verify IDREST plots and 3D canvas visible during simulation
21. Wait for simulation completion (Download CSV enabled, 300s timeout)
22. Verify Download CSV and Reconfigure enabled after completion
23. Verify success notification with View Simulation link
